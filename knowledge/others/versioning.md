## Software Release Life Cycle
- **Pre-alpha:** Very early development, often incomplete, experimental, and not meant for public use.
- **Alpha:** First somewhat testable version, but still very unstable and missing features.
- **Beta:** More complete; mainly testing and bug fixing. Still may have issues, but usable.
- **Release Candidate (RC):** Nearly final; only critical fixes remain.
- **Stable/Release:** Official finished version.
